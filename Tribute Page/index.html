Norbyne Litaoen

A woman. A wife. A mom. A friend. A colleague.

One of the superheroes of Destiny Litaoen

A timeline of Norbyne Litaoen's life so far:

  • 1972 - Born in Baguio City, Philippines
  • 1989 - Entered Saint Louis University under B.S. Electronics and Communication Engineering
  • 1994 - Graduated from SLU and finished her Bachelor's Degree, gaining a license for Aircraft Mechanic / Specialist License
  • 1995 - Left her family to work abroad in Taiwan; Worked with Acer and Intel Orient Semiconductor Electronics Ltd.; Helped pay for the schooling of her younger siblings
  • 1997 - Came back to the Philippines; Hired as an assembly technician by Moog Controls Corp. in Baguio City
  • 1999 - Promoted to Quality Assurance Auditor, becoming the first female to be in that job position; Earned certifications from Boeing and Joint Aviation Authority
  • 2001 - Got married to Alfred Litaoen
  • 2002 - Her favorite eldest daughter, Alby Destiny, was born
  • 2003 - Her favorite youngest daughter, Tashza Diamond, was born
  • 2006 - Promoted to Program Manager, again, becoming the first female to be in that job position; Earned plenty of international certifications from Air Agency Certificate, European Aviation Safety Agency, Federal Aviation Administration, and Civil Aviation Authority of Singapore; Coordinated with the civil aviation authorities of Indonesia, Thailand, and Malaysia
  • 2009 - Earned a certification from the Civil Aviation Authority of Singapore; Coordinated with the civil aviation authorities of Indonesia, Thailand, Malaysia, and China
  • 2014 - Promoted to Manufacturing Manager
  • 2015 - Got Promoted and became the first female Business Unit Manager
  • 2016 - Promoted to Human Resources Manager
